Questions

FAQ

Straight answers before you buy or book.

Is this a template or a setup?
Both — you choose. Buy a template and set it up yourself with the included guide, or request Done-For-You setup and we install, connect, and brand everything for you. Templates are instant; DFY is hands-off.
Can you customize it?
Yes. Every DFY package includes adapting the system to your workflow and branding. If you need something designed completely from scratch, the Custom Build package covers that — scoped on a short call first.
Do I need coding?
No. Templates run on simple, familiar tools — no coding, no complex apps. For DFY setups, we handle 100% of the technical work and hand it over with a plain-language walkthrough.
Can I pay one time?
Yes. Templates and DFY setups are one-time payments — you own the system forever. Monthly plans exist only if you want ongoing support, and they're completely optional.
Do you offer monthly support?
Yes — three plans. Care Plan keeps your system healthy, Growth Plan improves it every month, and Operator Plan gives you hands-on operational support. Cancel anytime; your system stays yours.
Can I use my own branding?
Absolutely. Templates are built to be branded — your name, colors, and logo. In every DFY package, applying your branding is part of the setup.
How long does setup take?
Templates: same day — download and follow the guide. DFY setups typically go live within 1–5 business days depending on the package. Custom Builds get a timeline during the scope call before any payment.
